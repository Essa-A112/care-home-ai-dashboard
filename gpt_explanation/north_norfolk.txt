North Norfolk has shown promising potential for care home investment with an estimated score of 16.38, categorizing it as a 'Good' prospect. This district exhibits several positive indicators such as a robust house price growth rate of 15.09%, a high percentage of CQC rated 'Good' care homes at 61.02%, and a significant elderly population of 34.18%. Additionally, the relatively high number of care homes per 10,000 residents at 5.72 further supports its investment appeal. Despite the positive aspects, there are areas for improvement, notably the percentage of CQC requiring improvement at 32.2%. However, considering the overall positive metrics and the potential for growth in the care home sector, North Norfolk presents a suitable opportunity for investment. With the right strategies in place to address the areas requiring improvement, this district has the potential to further enhance its care home investment attractiveness and deliver favorable returns.
