The Local Authority District of Canterbury has been assessed with an estimated care home investment potential score of 7.93, placing it within the "Consider" category. This score is influenced by several key factors. Canterbury exhibits a moderate house price growth rate, which may present a challenge for potential investors. However, the district boasts a high percentage of care homes with a "Good" rating by the Care Quality Commission (CQC), indicating a strong level of care quality. Additionally, the significant proportion of residents aged 65 and over suggests a potential demand for care services. While there is room for improvement in the percentage of care homes requiring improvement, the overall number of care homes per capita is relatively favorable. Considering these factors, Canterbury falls within the category for potential investment after some enhancements. With a balanced mix of strengths and weaknesses, this district shows promise for care home investment with strategic improvements to enhance its overall attractiveness to investors.
