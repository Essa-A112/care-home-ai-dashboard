However, the presence of 11.43% CQC Requires Improvement ratings and a high percentage of CQC Good ratings at 77.14% suggest areas that may need improvement. Overall, while St Albans shows promise with its positive house price growth and income levels, the presence of areas needing improvement suggests that further enhancements may be beneficial before considering significant investment.
