The Local Authority District of Mid Devon has shown promising potential for care home investment with an estimated score of 7.66, placing it in the "Consider" category. Several factors contribute to this score, including a positive house price growth percentage of 9.24%, indicating a favorable economic environment for investment. Additionally, the percentage of individuals aged 65 and above stands at 24.62%, suggesting a growing market for elderly care services. Furthermore, a high proportion of care homes in the area have been rated as "Good" by the Care Quality Commission at 70.97%, reflecting a strong quality of care provision. However, there are areas for improvement, such as the percentage of care homes requiring improvement at 19.35% and those rated as "Outstanding" at 9.68%. These factors pose risks that could affect the overall attractiveness of the investment. While Mid Devon falls into the category of consideration for investment, further enhancements in care home quality and outstanding ratings could potentially elevate its status for future investment suitability.
