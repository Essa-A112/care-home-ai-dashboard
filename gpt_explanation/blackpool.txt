However, challenges lie in the lower proportion of outstanding CQC-rated care homes and a below-average Gross Disposable Household Income per head. These factors indicate room for improvement in care quality and potential financial constraints for residents. Overall, Blackpool falls within the range for potential investment consideration, with opportunities for enhancement in care quality and financial support.
