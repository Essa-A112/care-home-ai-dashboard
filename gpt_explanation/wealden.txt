The Wealden Local Authority District has an estimated care home investment potential score of 38.40, placing it in the "Consider" category. This score is influenced by several key features. Wealden benefits from a relatively high percentage of the population aged 65 and above, indicating a potential demand for care services. Additionally, the area has seen positive house price growth, suggesting a stable and potentially prosperous economic environment. However, the district does have a notable percentage of care homes that require improvement according to CQC ratings, which could pose a risk to potential investors. While the high percentage of CQC-rated "Good" care homes is a strength, the need for improvement in some facilities may require attention before full investment. Overall, Wealden falls into the "Consider" category, indicating that with targeted improvements, it could become a suitable investment opportunity in the future.
