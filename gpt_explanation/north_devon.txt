This score is likely influenced by several key factors. Furthermore, with 26.18% of the population aged 65 and over, there is a clear demand for elderly care services. However, the absence of any care homes rated as "Inadequate" could potentially limit opportunities for improvement and expansion in the sector. Overall, while North Devon falls slightly below the threshold for direct investment suitability, its positive indicators make it a feasible candidate for consideration after targeted enhancements to further strengthen its care home investment potential.
