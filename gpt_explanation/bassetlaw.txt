The Local Authority District of Bassetlaw has shown promising indicators in its care home investment potential assessment with a score of 55.06, categorizing it as a good investment opportunity. Factors contributing to this positive evaluation include a relatively low percentage of care homes rated as requiring improvement by the Care Quality Commission (CQC) at 31.25%, coupled with a high percentage of homes rated as good at 62.5%. Additionally, the significant growth in house prices by 43.61% and a sizeable population of individuals aged 65 and above at 22.26% further enhance the appeal of investing in care homes within this district. Notably, the absence of care homes rated as inadequate presents a favorable risk profile for potential investors. Overall, Bassetlaw's strong performance across key metrics positions it as a suitable candidate for care home investment, offering a potentially lucrative opportunity for stakeholders looking to enter or expand in this market.
