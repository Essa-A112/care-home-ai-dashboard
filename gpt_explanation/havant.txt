The Local Authority District of Havant has shown promising indicators for care home investment potential with a score of 11.74, categorizing it as a good prospect for investment. The district boasts a relatively low percentage of care homes requiring improvement, standing at 31.03%, along with a high proportion of homes rated as 'Good' by the Care Quality Commission at 68.97%. Additionally, Havant exhibits a healthy house price growth rate of 8.84% and a significant population of 65 years and older at 24.86%. These factors collectively contribute to its positive score. However, the absence of care homes rated as 'Outstanding' could be seen as a potential area for growth. Overall, Havant's favorable demographic and quality of care home metrics position it as a suitable candidate for investment, offering potential opportunities for further development and enhancement within the care home sector.
