The Local Authority District of Wirral has shown a promising care home investment potential score of 51.10, categorizing it as a good investment opportunity. This score is primarily influenced by key positive factors such as a relatively low percentage of care homes requiring improvement (31.67%) and a high percentage of care homes rated as good by the Care Quality Commission (62.5%). Additionally, the significant house price growth of 9.19% contributes positively to the overall investment attractiveness of Wirral. While the percentage of inadequate care homes is relatively low at 5.83% and there are no outstanding-rated care homes, indicating a stable quality level, there may still be room for improvement in certain areas. Considering the overall positive indicators, Wirral presents itself as a suitable location for care home investment, offering a favorable balance of strengths and opportunities for potential investors.
