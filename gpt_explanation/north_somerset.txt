The Local Authority District of North Somerset has been assessed with an estimated care home investment potential score of 7.88, placing it in the "Consider" category. This score indicates moderate potential for investment in care homes within the district. North Somerset benefits from a healthy house price growth rate of 9.79%, suggesting a favorable economic environment for property investments. Additionally, the percentage of residents aged 65 and above is at 24.15%, indicating a potentially strong demand for care services in the area. However, the district does have a relatively high percentage of care homes requiring improvement according to CQC ratings, at 14.78%, which presents a challenge for investors seeking high-quality care facilities. Despite these weaknesses, the overall investment potential of North Somerset is promising, especially considering the moderate number of care homes per 10,000 residents, which signifies potential growth opportunities in the care home sector. Therefore, while North Somerset may require some improvements to fully capitalize on its investment potential, it remains a viable option for consideration by investors looking to enter the care home market.
