The Local Authority District of Warrington has an estimated care home investment potential score of 40.33, placing it in the "Consider" category. This score is influenced by several key factors. Warrington exhibits a robust House_Price_Growth_% of 26.8%, indicating a positive trend in the housing market that could attract potential investors. However, the district has relatively high percentages of care homes rated as requiring improvement (14.29%) and only 3.57% rated as outstanding by the Care Quality Commission (CQC), suggesting areas for enhancement in the quality of care services. On the positive side, Warrington has a notable proportion of individuals aged 65 and above at 19.66%, indicating a potential demand for care services in the area. Overall, while Warrington shows promise with its housing market growth and aging population, the need for improvement in care home quality raises considerations for investment. Therefore, further assessment and potential enhancements in care services could position Warrington as a more favorable investment opportunity in the future.
