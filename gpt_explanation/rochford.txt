The district also has a significant elderly population, with 23.26% aged 65 and over. However, Rochford faces challenges such as a decline in house price growth by 8.86%, indicating potential economic constraints. While there are no CQC-rated "Inadequate" care homes in the area, there is room for improvement to attract more investment. Overall, Rochford falls within the range where investment may be considered after improvements, suggesting potential for growth with targeted enhancements in various areas to make it more attractive for care home investments.
