Investing in care homes in the Local Authority of Arun shows promise with a potential return on investment of 17.53%. The area has a significant elderly population, with 28.82% aged 65 and over, indicating a demand for care services. The majority of care homes are rated as Good (76.4%), further highlighting the quality of care available, with a small percentage rated Outstanding (3.8%). Overall, Arun presents a viable opportunity for investment consideration in the care home sector.
