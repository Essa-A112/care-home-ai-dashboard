Investing in care homes in the New Forest Local Authority offers an attractive potential return on investment of 8.79%. With 30.3% of the population aged 65 and over, there is a strong demand for care services. Furthermore, the high percentage of care homes rated as Good (79.6%) and Outstanding (6.1%) indicates a favorable quality of care in the area. Overall, the New Forest presents a promising opportunity for investment in the care home sector.
