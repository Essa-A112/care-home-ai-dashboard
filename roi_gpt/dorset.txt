Investing in care homes in Dorset's Local Authority presents a promising opportunity with an attractive Return on Investment (ROI) of 11.69%. With 30.39% of the population aged 65 and over, there is a substantial demand for care facilities. Additionally, with 80.3% of care homes rated as Good and 4.5% as Outstanding, the quality of care services in the area is notably high. Considering these factors, Dorset is worth considering for investment in the care home sector.
