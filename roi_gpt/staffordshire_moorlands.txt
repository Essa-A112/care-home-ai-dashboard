Investing in care homes in Staffordshire Moorlands Local Authority could offer a solid return on investment of 12.96%. With over a quarter of the population aged 65 and above, there is a significant demand for care services. While 44.8% of care homes are rated as Good, there is room for improvement in achieving an Outstanding rating. Overall, the investment potential in this area is promising, especially for those looking for a stable and potentially profitable opportunity in the care home sector.
