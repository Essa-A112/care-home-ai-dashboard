Investing in care homes in the Local Authority of Bassetlaw offers a promising return on investment of 13.18%. With 22.26% of the population aged 65 and over, there is a strong demand for care services. Additionally, a high percentage of care homes in the area are rated as Good (62.5%) or Outstanding (6.2%), indicating a favorable quality of care. Overall, Bassetlaw presents a solid investment opportunity in the care home sector.
