Investing in care homes in the Local Authority of Norwich offers a potential Return on Investment (ROI) of 12.54%, making it a financially attractive option. With nearly 15% of the population aged 65 and over, there is a consistent demand for care services. Furthermore, over half of care homes are rated as Good, with 10.3% rated as Outstanding, indicating a solid quality of care within the area. Considering these factors, Norwich presents a promising opportunity for investment in the care home sector.
