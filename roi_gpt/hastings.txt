Investing in care homes in the Local Authority of Hastings shows promising potential with a high Return on Investment (ROI) of 24.45%. The area's significant population of individuals aged 65 and over at 20.8% indicates a strong demand for care services. Moreover, with 76.1% of care homes rated as Good and 1.4% as Outstanding, the quality of care facilities in the area is generally positive, making it a consideration for investment.
