The care home investment potential in Richmond upon Thames Local Authority shows promise with a Return on Investment (ROI) of 6.06%. Approximately 16.81% of the population is aged 65 and over, indicating a potential demand for care services. Moreover, a high percentage of care homes in the area are rated as Good or Outstanding (97.9%), reflecting a quality care provision. However, the area is graded as 'Avoid' for investment, suggesting caution may be warranted in considering this location for investment purposes.
