Investing in care homes in the Local Authority of Dover shows promising potential with a Return on Investment (ROI) of 9.86%. The area has a significant population aged 65 and over at 24.53%, indicating a demand for care services. With 72.1% of care homes rated as Good and 6.6% rated as Outstanding, the quality of care facilities is relatively high. Considering these factors, Dover is an investment grade location for those interested in the care home sector.
